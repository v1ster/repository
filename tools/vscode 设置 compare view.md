---
title: vscode 设置 compare view
date: 2023-05-10  
tags: vscode
---

1. 选择左边比较文件
	右键选择select for compare 
2. 选择右边比较文件
	右键选择compare with seleted
3. 设置比较视图
	按下 shitf + ctrl + p, 输入 compare .在下拉框中选择 compare: toggle inline view
